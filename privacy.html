Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you use Anikuvi, we may collect:

  • Basic usage data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Email addresses if you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Search queries for animal images
  • Non-personal analytics via cookies

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve animal image recommendations
  • Send conservation-related newsletters (if subscribed)
  • Analyze website traffic patterns
  • Prevent abuse of our services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited sharing may occur with:

  • Trusted analytics providers (Google Analytics)
  • Law enforcement if required by legal process

4. Image Sources

Animal images are sourced from:

  • Public APIs (Pexels, Unsplash)
  • Open-license wildlife photography
  • Original content from our team

All images are used in accordance with their respective licenses.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Unsubscribe from newsletters
  • Disable cookies via browser settings

6. Policy Updates

This policy was last updated on June 2024. Significant changes will be notified via our website.

For questions, contact: privacy@anikuvi.com
